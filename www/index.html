About me

I am Ossi Tuuttila, an architect student pursuing a Master’s degree in Architecture at the northernmost architecture school in the world, where my passion lies in sustainable design, cultural heritage preservation, and innovative spatial solutions.

My design approach is rooted in sustainability, renovation, and the preservation of cultural history. I am inspired by people who see the world not just as it is, but as it could be—and I strive to bring that vision into my work.
